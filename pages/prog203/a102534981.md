### Job Description
------------------------

<div class="post_content" itemprop="articleBody">
 <p>
  Job title: Accountant
 </p>
 <p>
  Principle Duties and Responsibilities:
 </p>
 <p>
  1.	Prepare, examine, or analyze accounting records, financial statements, or other financial reports to assess accuracy, completeness, and conformance to reporting and procedural standards.
  <br/>
  2.	Review accounts for discrepancies and reconcile differences.
  <br/>
  3.	Establish tables of accounts and assign entries to proper accounts.
  <br/>
  4.	Develop, implement, modify, and document recordkeeping and accounting systems, making use of current computer technology.
  <br/>
  5.	Oversee billing, accounts payable, timekeeping, payroll, GL reconciliations
  <br/>
  6.	Prepare forms and manuals for accounting and bookkeeping personnel and direct their work activities.
  <br/>
  7.	Survey operations to ascertain accounting needs and to recommend, develop, or maintain solutions to business and financial problems.
  <br/>
  8.	Advise clients in areas such as compensation, employee health care benefits, the design of accounting or data processing systems.
  <br/>
  9.	Processing of contract documents into the accounting system.
  <br/>
  10.	Producing required bank reports
  <br/>
  11.	Respond to auditor and external stakeholder requests
  <br/>
  12.	Maintaining and Tracking Departmental Budgets
  <br/>
  13.	Prepare and monitor budgets for special projects as assigned
  <br/>
  14.	Liaise with other New Tang Dynasty Television accounting office’s when necessary.
 </p>
 <p>
  Knowledge:
  <br/>
  Economics and Accounting — Knowledge of economic and accounting principles and practices, the financial markets, banking and the analysis and reporting of financial data.
  <br/>
  Mathematics — Knowledge of arithmetic, algebra, geometry, calculus, statistics, and their applications.
  <br/>
  Clerical — Knowledge of administrative and clerical procedures and systems such as word processing, managing files and records, stenography and transcription, designing forms, and other office procedures and terminology.
  <br/>
  English Language — Knowledge of the structure and content of the English language including the meaning and spelling of words, rules of composition, and grammar.
  <br/>
  Administration and Management — Knowledge of business and management principles involved in strategic planning, resource allocation, human resources modeling, leadership technique, production methods, and coordination of people and resources.
  <br/>
  Customer and Personal Service — Knowledge of principles and processes for providing customer and personal services. This includes customer needs assessment, meeting quality standards for services, and evaluation of customer satisfaction.
 </p>
 <p>
  Requirements:
 </p>
 <p>
  Bachelors in accounting, finance, economics, or other similar business-related fields
  <br/>
  At least 5 years working experience in accounting
  <br/>
  At least 2+ years’ experience working in a non-profit organization
  <br/>
  Good command of English, Mandarin and Cantonese
  <br/>
  Good command of Quickbooks Accounting
  <br/>
  Good command of EMG Accounting Reporting Software
  <br/>
  Good command of Socoms Financial Reporting System
  <br/>
  Honest, compassionate, patient, autonomous and hard working
 </p>
